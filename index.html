Flatbed Towing

Flatbed towing is one of the safest ways to transport vehicles, as it keeps all four wheels off the ground. This method is ideal for luxury cars, vintage vehicles, motorcycles, and cars with all-wheel drive. We use modern flatbed trucks to ensure secure and damage-free transportation.

Flatbed towing is particularly beneficial for vehicles with low ground clearance, as it prevents undercarriage damage. It also reduces wear and tear during transport, making it the preferred method for high-value and specialty vehicles.

When to Call a Towing Company

While some vehicle issues can be resolved on the spot, there are situations where towing is the safest and most practical solution. You should consider calling Javi’s Towing And Transport Orlando in the following situations:

  • Vehicle breakdown: If your car will not start, is making unusual noises, or shows signs of mechanical failure, it is best to call for a tow.
  • Accidents: After a collision, especially if the vehicle is damaged or unsafe to drive, towing ensures your car is safely transported.
  • Flat tires: If you do not have a spare tire or are unable to change it, towing may be necessary.
  • Engine failure: If your car overheats, stalls, or has transmission issues, towing is often the safest option.
  • Transporting a non-drivable vehicle: For classic cars, project vehicles, or cars awaiting repairs, professional towing ensures safe transport.

Tips for Staying Safe While Waiting for a Tow

If you find yourself stranded and waiting for Javi’s Towing And Transport Orlando, here are some safety tips to keep in mind:

  • Stay in your vehicle if it is safe: If you are on a busy road, keeping the doors locked is safer.
  • Turn on hazard lights: Make sure your hazard lights are on to alert other drivers.
  • Move to a safe location: Pull over to the shoulder or a nearby parking lot to avoid oncoming traffic.
  • Avoid accepting help from strangers: Waiting for a professional towing company you trust is best.
